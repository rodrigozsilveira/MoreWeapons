# MoreWeapons

MoreWeapons is a Fabric mod for **Minecraft 1.21+** that introduces balanced, vanilla-style weaponry. Each weapon type features unique mechanics and stats to diversify your combat experience.

##  Weapon Types

### 1. Daggers
*  **Increased Attack Speed**: Strike faster than a standard sword.
*  **Bleeding Effect**: Deals damage over time to your targets.
*  **Shorter Range**: Requires you to get up close and personal.

### 2. Katanas
*  **Dash Ability**: Move quickly through enemies.
*  **Standard Range**: Same reach as a vanilla sword.
*  **Agile**: Slightly higher attack speed than a sword.

### 3. Scythes
*  **Increased Range**: Keep your enemies at a distance.
*  **Wide Sweep**: Greatly increased sweep attack hitbox for crowd control.
*  **Slow Swing**: Decreased attack speed for balanced gameplay.

---

##  Netherite Upgrade
Upgrading to Netherite follows the modern vanilla standard. To upgrade your Diamond weapons, use the **Smithing Table** with:

1.  **Netherite Upgrade Smithing Template**
2.  Your **Diamond Weapon** (Dagger, Katana, or Scythe)
3.  **Netherite Ingot**
